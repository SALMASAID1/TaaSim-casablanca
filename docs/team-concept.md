## 1-Slide Startup Concept

### 🚕 TaaSim: The Brain Behind Urban Mobility

**Problem:** Casablanca's 4M citizens have zero digital taxi infrastructure — no GPS tracking, no demand visibility, no dynamic matching. Riders wait 15+ minutes; taxis cruise empty 40% of the time.

**Solution:** A real-time Big Data platform that:
- **Matches riders to taxis in < 5 seconds** using Apache Flink streaming
- **Predicts demand surges 30 minutes ahead** with Spark ML (GBT model)
- **Gives city planners a live dashboard** of all 16 arrondissements

**How:** Kappa Architecture — Kafka as the single event bus, Flink for real-time processing, Spark for batch analytics and ML, Cassandra for sub-2ms serving, Grafana for visualization.

**Market:** 50,000+ taxis in Morocco's top 5 cities. Revenue from per-ride commissions (2-5%) and city planning SaaS subscriptions.

**Ask:** Pilot with 100 Casablanca taxis + real GPS data access.
